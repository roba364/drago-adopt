Rescue story

A small dog with a big will to live.

Drago was spotted near a roundabout in Belgrade, darting between cars and nearly hit. Someone scooped him up and carried him to safety — but the story didn't end there.

The little one simply followed. Dirty, exhausted, covered in huge ticks, yet wearing a collar — as if he'd once had a home. He stayed at her heels for almost 35 minutes, walked about 3 km, and kept right on going beside her.

So he was taken to a vet clinic: no microchip, no owner to be found. Drago was examined, given his first vaccine, and moved to temporary foster — to rest, regain his strength, and wait for his real family.

He didn't ask for help. He simply chose to walk beside her.

Character

Soft, gentle and deeply attached to people.

Drago loves to be near you — lying at your feet, falling asleep beside you, following you around the home and being part of family life. A little companion: affectionate, attentive and very endearing.
