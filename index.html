Rescue story

A small dog with a big will to live

Drago was rescued in Belgrade near a roundabout. He followed his rescuer for a long way, got to a veterinary clinic, and has been learning how safe home life can be.

He was found alone on the street and made an enormous effort to stay close to the person who noticed him. After the vet check he moved into a temporary home, where he is now growing, resting, eating well, and slowly becoming a confident puppy.
