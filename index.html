Rescue story

A small dog with a big will to live.

Drago was found alone on the street near a roundabout in Belgrade. He made an enormous effort to stay close to the person who noticed him — walked an entire route by her side. After the vet check he moved into a temporary home, where he is now resting, eating well, gaining confidence, and slowly learning what safe home life feels like.

He didn't beg. He just walked beside her, like he already trusted she'd take him home.
